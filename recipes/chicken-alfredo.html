Chicken Alfredo

Description

This chicken alfredo recipe will make your mouth water. Delicious alfredo sauce coupled with naturally sourced ingredients make for an exquisite combination of taste and structure, resulting in the most pleasurable sensation that your taste buds could ever fathom.

This chicken alfredo recipe will make you never want to go back. You will never need another bologna sandwich recipe. This is it. There's no turning back now.

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles of your choosing (recommended linguine)
  • Alfredo sauce (we will be using store-bought)
  • Chicken breasts
  • Spices
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees celcius and once oven is heated up, place thawed chicken breasts on cooking rack and in oven
  2. Put one box of noodles (around 500g) in already-boiling water inside large pot
  3. Prepare desired spices, we recommend cayenne pepper and garlic powder
  4. Intermittently check pasta and chicken for desired temperature and consistency. Chicken should be a minimum of 165 degrees celcius internally, while pasta done-ness is more of a personal preference
  5. Once chicken is done, add spices to both sides and cut into reasonable-sized portions to be mixed in with the pasta
  6. Once pasta is done, strain water out of pot and add the pasta back into the pot.
  7. Add sauce and chicken to pot, mix and enjoy!
  8. And don't forget you can never have enough parmesan cheese
