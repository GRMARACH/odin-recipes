Bologna Sandwich

Description

This bologna sandwich recipe will make your mouth water. Hand-crafted white bread coupled with naturally sourced ingredients make for an exquisite combination of taste and structure, resulting in the most pleasurable sensation that your taste buds could ever fathom.

This bologna sandwich recipe will make you never want to go back. You will never need another bologna sandwich recipe. This is it. There's no turning back now.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Sliced cheese (of your choosing)
  • Bologna slices
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • Lettuce (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare two slices of bread from bread loaf
  2. Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each pieces of bread
  3. Place cheese directly on bread
  4. Add the bologna
  5. Add the lettuce
  6. Enjoy!
