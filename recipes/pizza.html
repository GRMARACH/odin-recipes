Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe will make your mouth water. Hand-crafted Italian dough coupled with naturally sourced ingredients make for an exquisite combination of taste and structure, resulting in the most pleasurable sensation that your taste buds could ever fathom

This pizza recipe will make you never want to go back. You will never need another pizza recipe. This is it. There's no turning back now.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Sausage
  • Donair Meat
  • Bacon
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Set oven to preheat at 450 degrees celcius
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Add and spread the sauce
  4. Add the cheese
  5. Add the meat
  6. Cook in oven for 20-25 minutes or until crust is golden brown
